Voting Experiment

May 2017

Below we summarize the results of a "large scale" email reminder campaign on voter participation, conducted as part of the Experiment and Causality class of the Berkeley degree.

More specifically, we sent an email containing a politically neutral reminder about Californiaâ€™s 34th congressional district special election to around 40,000 people. In parallel, we sent an email to 40,000 people on blood drive as a placebo.

We find no clear impact - both in terms of practical and statistical significance. We suggest several improvements to the methodology which could lead to more conclusive results.
