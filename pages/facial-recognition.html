Facial Keypoints Detection

December 2016

As part of a Berkeley class on Machine Learning, we entered a Kaggle tutorial competition on Facial Keypoints detection. The objective of is to predict keypoint positions on face images, which can be used in tracking faces in videos, analysing facial expressions, biometrics, etc.

The data consists of 7049 images of 96 by 96 pixels. There are 15 facial keypoints to identify, each with 2 coordinates. The dataset is only fully labelled for ~2225 images, with the others only containing 4 features (2 eye centers, nose tip, outh center bottom lip)
