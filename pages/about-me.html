About Me


My name is Guillaume De Roo.

I grew up in France, where I studied to be an engineer, with a deep emphasis on Mathematics. After toying with finance and nuclear science, I went into consulting where I have worked for the last 6 years.

The rise of Data Science and Machine Learning have proven have sparked my interest, so I started taking classes on the side, first at Stanford and Coursera in 2015, and then more formally at Berkeley and Udacity.

I moved to San Francisco in July 2014 to be closer to the tech industry and my friends. Before that I had spent 2.5 years in São Paulo / Rio de Janeiro, and 2 years in Boston for my graduate studies.
