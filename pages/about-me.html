About Me


My name is Guillaume De Roo.

I spent most of my life in France, where I studied to be an engineer, with a deep emphasis on Applied Mathematics. After toying with finance and nuclear science, I went into consulting where I have spent the last 6 years.

The rise of Data Science and Machine Learning have proven too hard to resist, so I started taking classes on the side, first at Stanford and Coursera, and then more formally at Berkeley and Udacity.

I moved to San Francisco in July 2014 after spending 2-3 years in Brazil between São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro. Before that I had spent 2 years in Boston, during my graduate studies at MIT.
